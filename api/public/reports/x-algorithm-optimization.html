2. Tweet Composition (Content Optimization)

What BOOSTS Visibility

Engagement Velocity Critical

WHY: Algorithm tracks FavoritedByCountFeature, RetweetedByCountFeature, RepliedByCountFeature

ACTION: Optimize for immediate engagement in first 30-60 minutes

TACTICS: Post when your audience is most active, ask engaging questions, use conversation starters

Quality Content Signals Critical

WHY: GrokIsLowQualityFeature, GrokSunnyScoreFeature, and content understanding models affect ranking

ACTION: Create substantive, original content that provides genuine value

SPECIFIC: Long-form threads explaining complex AI/crypto concepts

AVOID: Low-effort posts, copy-paste content, pure promotional material

Video Content High

WHY: TweetVideoWatch signal is heavily weighted, ViewCountsFeatureHydrator tracks video engagement

ACTION: Include video content when possible, especially for technical explanations

OPTIMIZE: First 3 seconds are critical for retention metrics

In-Network Engagement Critical

WHY: InNetworkFavoritesCount, InNetworkRetweetsCount features prioritize engagement from followers

ACTION: Focus on building genuine follower relationships, not just follower count

STRATEGY: Engage authentically with your existing followers' content

What KILLS Reach

Spam/Low Quality Detection Critical

WHY: GrokIsSpamFeature, GrokIsLowQualityFeature actively filter content

AVOID:

  • Excessive hashtags (algorithm can detect hashtag spam)
  • Repetitive content patterns
  • Overly promotional language
  • Copy-paste responses

NSFW Content Critical

WHY: GrokNsfwFilter, GrokIsNsfwFeature, GrokIsSoftNsfwFeature filter content

AVOID: Any content that could trigger NSFW detection

BE CAREFUL: Some crypto/meme content might get flagged

Negative Feedback Signals Critical

WHY: FeedbackFatigueFilter removes content from users who clicked "See Fewer"

MONITOR: Track replies that suggest fatigue ("stop posting about this")

ADJUST: If getting negative feedback, pivot content strategy

Misinformation/Toxicity Critical

WHY: pToxicity, pAbuse models actively filter toxic content

AVOID: Controversial political takes, aggressive language, unsubstantiated claims

SAFE: Stick to technical/educational content about AI/crypto

Optimal Tweet Structure

Length & Format Medium

WHY: User engagement patterns affect TweetClick and dwell time signals

OPTIMAL:

  • Single tweets: 100-280 characters with clear hook
  • Threads: 3-5 tweets maximum for technical explanations
  • Include 1-2 line breaks for readability

Media Attachments Impact High

WHY: NumImagesFeature tracked, different media types have different engagement patterns

IMAGES: High engagement, but ensure they're relevant and high-quality
VIDEOS: Highest algorithmic boost due to watch time metrics
LINKS: Can reduce reach initially, but valuable for driving traffic
STRATEGY: Lead with native content, follow up with link in replies

Hashtags: Help or Hurt? Medium

WHY: Not explicitly tracked in core ranking features, but affects discovery

OPTIMAL: 1-3 relevant hashtags maximum

EFFECTIVE: #AI, #crypto, #blockchain, #DeFi (when relevant)

AVOID: Hashtag stuffing, trending hashtags unrelated to content

Threading Strategy High

WHY: ConversationFeature, thread structure affects engagement distribution

STRUCTURE: Hook tweet → explanation tweets → conclusion/CTA

TIMING: Post full thread quickly (within 5-10 minutes)

ENGAGEMENT: First tweet performance determines thread reach

3. Engagement & Supporting Activity

Reply Strategy Critical

WHY: TweetReply signal heavily weighted, RepliedByCountFeature affects ranking

PRIORITY: Reply to high-engagement accounts in AI/crypto space

QUALITY: Substantial replies that add value, not just agreements

TIMING: Early replies to viral tweets get more visibility

AVOID: Generic "great post" replies that signal low quality

Quote Tweet vs Retweet Behavior High

WHY: Retweet and QuoteTweet are separate signals, quote tweets generate more engagement

QUOTE TWEETS: Add your perspective, builds your thought leadership

RETWEETS: Use sparingly, mainly for content you strongly endorse

RATIO: Aim for 70% original, 20% quote tweets, 10% retweets

Timing & Frequency High

WHY: Engagement velocity affects ranking, but FeedbackFatigueFilter punishes overposting

FREQUENCY: 2-5 tweets per day maximum for main content

TIMING: When your crypto/AI audience is active (typically US afternoons/evenings)

SPACING: Space posts by 2+ hours to avoid fatigue

Algorithm's Engagement Scoring Critical

WHY: Different engagement types have different weights in ranking

HIERARCHY (highest to lowest impact):

  1. Replies with high engagement
  2. Quote tweets
  3. Retweets
  4. Likes
  5. Clicks
  6. Impressions

OPTIMIZE: Create content that encourages replies and discussions

Supporting Tweet Activity Medium

WHY: UserActionSequence suggests behavior patterns are tracked

ENGAGEMENT: Regularly engage with others' content in your niche

CONSISTENCY: Maintain steady activity levels (avoid long gaps)

AUTHENTICITY: Genuine engagement patterns vs. artificial boosting

4. Algorithm Internals

"For You" Feed Ranking Critical

HOW IT WORKS:

  1. Candidate Generation: Pulls from multiple sources (Earlybird search, UTEG, Follow Recommendations)
  2. Feature Hydration: Adds ~6000 features for each candidate tweet
  3. Heavy Ranking: ML model scores tweets based on predicted engagement
  4. Filtering: Removes spam, NSFW, duplicate content
  5. Mixing: Combines with ads and other content

OPTIMIZATION: Content must pass ALL stages - high-quality source material → strong features → high ML score → pass filters

Trust Scores & Author Reputation Critical

WHY: tweepcred (PageRank for users) and various author features affect ranking

TRACKED METRICS:

  • Account age and verification status
  • Follower-to-following ratio
  • Engagement rates on historical tweets
  • Negative feedback history

BUILD TRUST:

  • Consistent expertise in your domain
  • Regular engagement without aggressive behavior
  • Avoid actions that trigger spam detection

Content Filtering & Demotion Signals Critical

HARD FILTERS (Complete removal):

  • NSFW content (GrokNsfwFilter)
  • Spam detection (GrokIsSpamFeature)
  • Safety violations (trust_and_safety_models)
  • Feedback fatigue (FeedbackFatigueFilter)

SOFT DEMOTION (Reduced reach):

  • Low quality content (GrokIsLowQualityFeature)
  • Repetitive posting patterns
  • Low engagement rates
  • Negative feedback accumulation

Twitter Blue/Premium Impact High

WHY: Multiple verification features tracked separately in ranking model

DIRECT: Verification status is a ranking feature

INDIRECT: Blue subscribers get higher rate limits, priority support

RECOMMENDED: Essential for serious business accounts

Community Detection (SimClusters) High

HOW IT WORKS: Algorithm groups users into ~145K communities based on follow patterns

YOUR COMMUNITY: Algorithm places you in crypto/AI communities based on who follows you

OPTIMIZATION: Engage with other respected AI/crypto accounts to strengthen community signals

CROSS-POLLINATION: Occasionally engage with adjacent communities (tech, startups) for reach expansion

Immediate Reach Killers

  • Aggressive promotion - Pure promotional content without value

Long-term Trust Damage

  • Misinformation - Unsubstantiated claims about AI/crypto
